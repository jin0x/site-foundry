# Privacy Policy

Last updated: **2026-04-19**

We collect the minimum information required to operate the Site Foundry workspace and provide support when you use the operator API.

## What we collect

- **Account information** — email, workspace name, and role.
- **Operational metadata** — generation runs, artifact paths, and connector results.
- **Support correspondence** — any message you send us when you ask for help.

We do not sell this information to third parties. We share it only with the sub-processors listed below.

## How we use it

1. To authenticate operators against the workspace.
2. To run generation pipelines and store their artifacts.
3. To diagnose failures and improve reliability over time.

## Your choices

You can request export or deletion of your account data at any time. We process requests within a reasonable window and confirm completion in writing.

> If you have a specific concern, contact us at [privacy@example.com](mailto:privacy@example.com). We will respond within five business days.

## Changes to this policy

We revise this policy when we add features, change sub-processors, or update our retention practices. Material changes trigger a notification to active operators.
